Seal

Seal is a secure file management app designed to enhance user privacy by encrypting files on the device before they are uploaded to cloud storage services such as Google Drive and OneDrive. This approach ensures that users' files remain encrypted and secure while stored in the cloud, accessible only with the proper decryption keys held by the user.

Download

How it Works

Seal integrates smoothly with popular cloud storage providers, allowing users to manage and store their encrypted files in familiar environments like Google Drive and OneDrive without compromising security or ease of use

End-to-End Encryption

Seal encrypts files locally on the user’s device before uploading them to third-party cloud storage (like Google Drive and OneDrive), ensuring files remain private and secure

Minimal Permissions

Seal requests only the necessary permissions to manage encrypted files in the user’s cloud storage, reducing potential privacy risks by limiting access to other data.

User-Controlled Access

With Seal, users retain full control over their files and decryption keys, meaning only they can access their unencrypted data, even while it's stored in the cloud.
