Privacy Policy

Welcome to Seal! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Seal app, a secure file management tool that encrypts files before uploading them to third-party cloud storage services like Google Drive and OneDrive. By using Seal, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect : Google Drive and OneDrive Access Seal requests limited access to your Google Drive and/or OneDrive accounts to allow file uploads, downloads, and management of encrypted files within your cloud storage. We collect and store only the necessary access tokens required to enable these services. App-Specific Data Seal may store settings, configurations, and other app-specific data within Google Drive's hidden app data folder or within the app itself. This data is encrypted and kept secure, accessible only to the Seal app.

2. How We Use Your Information : File Management Seal encrypts your files locally on your device before uploading them to your cloud storage provider. We do not have access to the contents of your files, as encryption is performed directly on your device. The app only interacts with files created by Seal unless additional permissions are explicitly granted by you. Improving App Functionality App-specific data may be used to enhance and personalize your experience within Seal, such as saving preferences or configurations securely.

3. How We Protect Your Information : Encryption: All files are encrypted on your device before upload, ensuring that neither Seal nor third-party services have access to unencrypted data. Minimal Permissions: Seal requests only the necessary permissions required to function, including limited Google Drive scopes for secure file management and app data storage. Secure Storage: All access tokens and app-specific data are securely stored on your device and are not accessible by any third-party services outside the permissions you approve.

4. Sharing Your Information : Seal does not share, sell, or rent your personal data or files with any third parties. Your files are managed solely within your Google Drive or OneDrive accounts, and we do not have access to these accounts beyond the permissions you explicitly grant.

5. Data Retention : Seal does not retain or store your files on our servers. All encrypted files remain within your cloud storage account, under your control.
